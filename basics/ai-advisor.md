# 🤖 AI Advisor

The AI Advisor is your personal automotive mentor — powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash and calibrated to your specific vehicle, verified build history, and goals. It's not a generic chatbot. It knows your car.

***

## What It Does

* Suggests modifications based on your build stage and goals
* Flags compatibility issues between mods and your specific vehicle
* Estimates cost ranges for suggested modifications
* Recommends a logical build sequence so mods don't conflict
* Explains complex automotive concepts in plain language — gear ratios, suspension geometry, boost curves
* Analyzes photos you upload to identify wear, suggest fixes, or decode dashboard warning lights
* Searches the web live to find verified part listings from trusted retailers

***

## How to Use It

Open the **AI Advisor** tab and type your question or goal:

> *"I want to add 50whp without touching the engine"*
> *"Best suspension setup for track days on a stock 2020 WRX"*
> *"What does this warning light mean?"* ← attach a photo

The advisor responds with recommendations specific to your verified vehicle and current build stage.

***

## Multimodal — Photo Analysis

The Advisor can analyze images you upload directly in the chat:

- 📸 Upload a photo of engine wear, a damaged part, or a warning light
- 🔍 The Advisor identifies the issue and explains it in plain terms
- 🔧 Suggests next steps, repair options, or compatible replacement parts

***

## Live Web Search

The Advisor has access to live web search. When you ask about parts or modifications, it can pull real-time results from trusted retailers — returning verified product URLs so you can buy directly from the source.
