# 🔧 Accessories

Accessories are virtual items used to customize your Car Card. They are obtained exclusively through Car Packs.

***

## Accessory Types

| Type           | Description                                                  |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 🖼️ Backgrounds  | The backdrop of your Car Card — from clean gradients to themed environments |
| 🔲 Frames       | Borders and edge styling around your card                    |
| 🏅 Badges       | Icons and emblems displayed on your card                     |
| 🎨 Stickers     | Decorative overlays and decals                               |
| ✨ Effects      | Animated visual effects applied to your card                 |

***

## Managing Your Collection

View all owned accessories from your **Profile → Collection** tab. Equip or swap accessories at any time — changes apply instantly to your Car Card across the platform.

***

## Rarity

Each accessory has a rarity tier (Common through Legendary) that determines how rare it is to obtain. Higher-tier packs increase your chances of pulling rarer accessories.
