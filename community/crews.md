# 👥 Crews

Crews are Carbase AI's community hubs — built for car people who want to connect, compete, and build together. Every Crew is organized around a vehicle segment or culture.

***

## Crew Categories

When joining or creating a Crew, you select a category that defines the community's identity:

| Category | Focus |
| ------------ | ----- |
| 🇯🇵 JDM | Japanese domestic market builds |
| 🇪🇺 EURO | European performance and luxury |
| 🦅 MUSCLE | American V8 and classic muscle |
| ⚡ EV | Electric vehicle builds and conversions |
| 🏔️ OFF\_ROAD | Trail rigs, overlanders, lifted trucks |
| 🏁 TRACK | Time attack, autocross, road course builds |
| 🚗 DAILY | Street-driven, functional builds |
| 🎨 AESTHETIC | Show builds, stance, and visual culture |
| 🔩 OEM\_PLUS | Factory-correct with tasteful upgrades |

***

## Joining a Crew

Browse available Crews from the **Community** tab and tap **Join**. Some Crews are open to anyone; others require an invite from an existing member.

***

## Inside a Crew

### 💬 Crew Chat

Real-time messaging with all Crew members. Share updates, coordinate meetups, hype builds, and talk shop.

### 🚗 Build Sharing

Share your Car Card and full vehicle specs with the Crew. Members can see your OVR Score, mod list, build timeline, and Class Rating.

### 📍 Meetup Check-Ins

Attend real-world meetups and check in through the app. Every check-in is logged to your profile and contributes to your Crew's activity standing.

### 🏆 Daily Group Challenge

Each Crew receives a rotating **Daily Group Challenge** — cooperative tasks that require multiple members to complete together. Completing a challenge unlocks massive XP bonuses for all participating members.

### 🔥 Trending Mods

The platform scans every Car Card in your Crew and surfaces the **top 3 trending parts** inside that community — showing what the most active builders are actually running right now.

***

## Crew Leaderboard

Each Crew has an internal leaderboard ranking members by OVR Score. Every time a member's OVR Score updates, the leaderboard recalculates in real time. The higher your build level, the higher your standing within the Crew.

Member activity also feeds **Crew XP** — contributing to the Crew's overall leaderboard standing across the platform.
