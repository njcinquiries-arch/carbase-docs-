# 👥 Crews

Crews are Carbase AI's community groups — built for car people who want to connect beyond just sharing photos.

***

## Joining a Crew

Browse available Crews from the **Community** tab and tap **Join**. Some Crews are open to anyone; others may require an invite from an existing member.

***

## Inside a Crew

### 💬 Crew Chat

Talk to members, share updates, hype builds, and coordinate — all within the Crew's dedicated chat.

### 🚗 Build Sharing

Share your Car Card and full vehicle specs with your Crew. Let other members see your OVR Score, mod list, and build timeline.

### 📍 Meetup Check-Ins

Attend real-world meetups and check in through the app to log your presence. Build your meetup history and rep within your Crew.

***

## Crew Leaderboard

Each Crew has an internal leaderboard ranking members by OVR Score. The higher your build level, the higher your rank within the Crew.

***

## 🔮 Coming Soon — Crew vs. Crew

We're developing a **Crew vs. Crew card game mode** where Crews can compete head-to-head using their Car Cards. This feature is in development and will be announced when it's ready.
