# 🔭 Discover

Discover is the global build index — a swipe-based feed where you explore Car Cards from builders across the entire Carbase AI community.

***

## Swipe Mode

Browse builds one at a time:

* Swipe **up** ↑ to like a build
* Swipe **down** ↓ to pass

Once your daily swipe quota is exhausted, the stack locks with a countdown to the next reset.

***

## Grid View

Switch to **Grid View** to see multiple builds at a glance. Tap any card to open it in full detail — specs, mod log, OVR Score breakdown, and build timeline.

***

## Daily Swipe Limits

| Plan | Daily Swipes |
| ------------ | ------------ |
| Free | 10 per day |
| CarBase Pro | Unlimited |
| VIP | Unlimited |

***

## What You'll Find

Verified builds from the community, surfaced by OVR Score, build activity, and verification status. The more complete, documented, and verified a build is — the more visibility it earns on Discover.

VIN-verified cards display a **Verified Owner** badge. Unverified cards are marked accordingly — the community always knows what's been confirmed.
