# 🔭 Discover & Curate: Inside The Vault

Welcome to The Vault — Carbase AI's global community showroom. This is where the world's most unique builds, daily projects, and custom tuner specs are showcase-ready. Whether you're looking for design inspiration, planning your next build checklist, or showing off your own card, the Vault is your hub for discovery.

***

## Choose Your View

Everyone browses differently. The Vault offers three tailored layout modes to help you explore builds your way.

### 📱 Swipe Mode — The Discover Stack

Swipe Mode stacks custom car cards in a clean, interactive deck that imitates a real, physical card stack.

* **The Experience:** Displays one car card at a time, letting you focus fully on the build's aesthetic, OVR Score, and unique modification theme.
* **Best For:** Quick, high-focus discovery and rating new community drops.

### 📋 Scroll Mode — Feed & Database

Tap the layout toggle to switch to a classic vertical scrolling experience. Two sub-modes available:

**1. Grid View (Dense Database)**
Presents cars in a clean multi-column catalog. Perfect for searching specific models, scanning dozens of builds quickly, or sorting by role category (JDM, Off-Road, Euro, etc.).

**2. Large View (Visual Social Feed)**
Displays cards in a large, expanded feed. Perfect for scrolling through full detail specs, seeing active modifications, and checking out owner profiles directly in your feed.

***

## Swipe Deck Gestures

In Swipe Mode, curating your feed is as simple as flicking the card:

| Gesture | Action | Result |
| ------- | ------ | ------ |
| Swipe Up 🟢 | Save to Collection | Card flashes green — saved to your personal Collection for future reference |
| Swipe Down 🔴 | Dismiss | Card flashes red — cleared from your discovery queue |
| Swipe Left or Right ⚪ | Pass | Card moves aside — next build in the stack loads |

***

## Tuner Spec Cloning *(PRO)*

See a build in the Vault and want to recreate it? With PRO, you can clone any build's parts list directly to your own card.

* **How It Works:** Open any build, tap **Clone**, and copy their entire parts list to your own car card in one tap.
* **Planned Upgrades Only:** To keep ratings honest, cloned specs are added as **Planned Modifications** — not verified mods. This acts as your personal parts wishlist, letting you map out your build budget and project goals before committing to the real thing.

### Free Member Cooldown

Once you've viewed 10 cards, the Vault enters a **5-hour cooldown**. A countdown screen displays exactly when your next batch is ready. You can continue browsing and inspecting cards already loaded in your active stack until the timer resets — you're never fully locked out mid-session.

***

## What You'll Find

Verified builds from the community, surfaced by OVR Score, build activity, and verification status. The more complete, documented, and verified a build is — the more visibility it earns in the Vault.

VIN-verified cards display a **✅ Verified Owner** badge. Unverified cards are marked with a **⚠️ Unverified Specs & Owner** banner — the community always knows what's been confirmed.
