# ⭐ OVR Score

The OVR Score is Carbase AI's proprietary rating system that measures your vehicle's overall build level, modification depth, and performance intent.

***

## How It's Calculated

Your OVR Score is based on:

* Factory specs and trim level (baseline)
* Verified modifications added to your build log
* Modification categories (performance, aesthetic, functional)
* Build completeness and documentation quality

**Baseline Score** — Your vehicle's score straight from the factory. Every car starts here.

**Current Score** — Your live OVR Score reflecting all logged and verified modifications.

***

## Modification Timeline

Every mod you add is timestamped and logged to your build history. Your OVR Score timeline shows exactly how your build has evolved — from stock to where it is today.

***

## Important Disclaimer

The OVR Score is an estimate and proprietary opinion metric — not a certified appraisal, safety rating, or financial valuation. Do not rely on it for purchase, sale, insurance, or financing decisions.
