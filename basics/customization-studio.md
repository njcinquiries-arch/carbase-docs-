# 🎨 Customization Studio

The Customization Studio is the creative suite where you personalize your Car Card — the visual identity of your vehicle on the platform.

***

## Card Skins

Card skins are custom backdrops, overlays, and graphics applied directly to the **face and backing of your Car Card**. Every skin is cataloged by rarity tier.

| Rarity | Skin Types |
| --------- | ----------- |
| ⚪ Common | Solid colors, basic matte textures, clean gradient meshes |
| 🟢 Uncommon | Brushed metal, gloss finishes, subtle pattern overlays |
| 🔵 Rare | Anodized metallic wraps, carbon-fiber weave, JDM cherry blossom underlays, themed graphics |
| 🟣 Epic | Animated gradient shifts, iridescent color-flip finishes, deep chrome layers |
| 🟠 Legendary | Liquid Gold finishes, holographic chrome, full animated shader matrices |

Legendary and Grail skins are rendered using live shader technology — they move, shift, and react to your screen in real time.

***

## Active Glows

Configure the ambient underglow that radiates from behind your card edges. Choose your glow color and intensity — it renders as a responsive vector gradient that frames the entire card.

***

## HUD Spec Simulator

As you toggle modifications in the Studio, the **Live HUD updates in real time:**

- Recalculates your current Horsepower and Torque figures
- Re-evaluates your Performance Class Rating (D through S)
- Updates the card face instantly — no save required to preview

This is a live simulation. What you see on screen is exactly what your card looks like to the community.
