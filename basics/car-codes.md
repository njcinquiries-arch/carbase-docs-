# 🚗 Car Codes

A Car Code is a unique verified identifier assigned to your vehicle on the Carbase AI platform.

Think of it as a digital title for your build — it ties your OVR Score, modification history, build photos, and Car Card to a single verifiable record linked to your VIN.

***

## What Car Codes Are Used For

* Establishing verified ownership of a build on the platform
* Displaying your verified build record to the community
* Transferring your build history if you sell your vehicle
* Unlocking platform features tied to vehicle verification

***

## How to Get One

Car Codes are available for purchase within the app via Apple In-App Purchase. All sales are final and non-refundable.
