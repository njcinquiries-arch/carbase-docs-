# 🔭 Discover

Discover is where you explore builds from the Carbase AI community and find vehicles that inspire you.

***

## Swipe Mode

Browse cars one at a time:

* Swipe **up** ↑ to like a build
* Swipe **down** ↓ to pass

***

## Grid View

Prefer to browse at a glance? Switch to **Grid View** to see multiple builds at once and tap into any card that catches your eye.

***

## Daily Swipe Limits

| Plan        | Daily Swipes |
| ----------- | ------------ |
| Free        | 5 per day    |
| Carbase Pro | Unlimited    |

***

## What You'll Find

Verified builds from the community, sorted by activity and OVR Score. The more complete and documented a build is, the more visibility it gets on Discover.
