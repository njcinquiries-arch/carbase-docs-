# 🤖 AI Advisor

The AI Advisor is your personal build consultant — trained on automotive data and calibrated to your specific vehicle, current mods, and goals.

***

## What It Does

* Suggests modifications based on your build stage and goals
* Flags compatibility issues between mods and your vehicle
* Estimates cost ranges for suggested modifications
* Recommends a logical build sequence so mods don't conflict
* Answers questions about your specific car

***

## How to Use It

Open the **AI Advisor** tab, type your question or goal — for example:

> *"I want to add 50whp without touching the engine"*
> *"Best suspension setup for track days on a stock 2020 WRX"*

The advisor responds with recommendations specific to your verified vehicle.
